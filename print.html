Arts. The well-rounded sub-board of print design, Arts is a platform that offers clear style rules and structure while giving designers some room to innovate. Designers lay out pages, manipulate photos, and create vector graphics for this twice-a-week part of the daily paper.

Daily. The steady, reliable backbone of The Crimson, Daily is the sub-board with the most structure and organization. Designers will gain experience in newspaper production and page layout, but they will also be able to make data visualizations, manipulate photos, and create vector graphics.

Fifteen Minutes. The "Wild West" of print design, FM is the sub-board with the least amount of style rules and the most freedom for creativity. Designers lay out pages, manipulate photos, create vector graphics, and make illustrations to accompany the articles in this fortnightly magazine.
